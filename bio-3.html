  • Simulation of Bile Action

    • Explain the role of bile in digestion. Is it involved in chemical or mechanical digestion?
      • Bile is essential for fat digestion since it is generated by the liver and stored in the gallbladder. Bile works as a mechanical facilitator in the small intestine by emulsifying big fat globules into smaller droplets, improving the surface area to volume ratio. This mechanical process improves the efficiency of lipases, which are enzymes that break down lipids into fatty acids and glycerol during chemical digestion. Bile's emulsification role, in essence, optimizes both mechanical and chemical elements of fat digestion.
