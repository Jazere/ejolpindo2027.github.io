Extra-Curriculars:

Welcome to Lectio Science High School's Extra-curricular Enclave, where the mystical and magical pursuits of knowledge reach far beyond the classroom! The diverse tapestry of extracurricular activities at our school provides students with a magical universe to explore, encouraging creativity, companionship, and a love of the arcane.

Specialization Years

The Alchemy Alcove

Bring forth your inner sorcerer! In the Alchemy Alcove, students learn about potion-making, transmutation, and the magical skill of changing the ordinary into the remarkable. This club is where science meets magic, with seething cauldrons and dazzling elixirs.
Specialization Years

The Enchanted Writing Guild

Write down your spells and words! The Enchanted Writing Guild is a paradise for storytellers, poets, and wordsmiths. Dive into the world of mysterious sonnets, wonderful stories, and enthralling poems. Allow your imagination to soar by immersing yourself in the literary arts.
Specialization Years

Astrology Assembly

Examine the cosmic secrets! Students are invited to attend the Astrology Assembly to learn about the stars, planets, and celestial occurrences. Investigate the ancient art of divination and delve into the supernatural forces that influence our fate. It's a celestial voyage that will leave you speechless!
Specialization Years

Spellbound Science Olympiad

Unravel the science-based magic! The Spellbound Science Olympiad is a series of enchanted competitions where students put their magical expertise to the test. This is where science becomes a mesmerising show, from mystical experiments to enthralling presentations.
Specialization Years

Quill and Quirk Literary Tournament

Allow your words to create magic! The Quill and Quirk Literary Tournament honours literature in all of its forms. Engage in enthralling debates, weave beautiful stories, and compose mystical sonnets that reflect the essence of Lectio Science High School.
Specialization Years

Magical Masquerade Ball

Join us for an evening of magic and mystery in your magical clothing! The Magical Masquerade Ball is an annual event when students may dance under the glittering stars, surrounded by our magical school's beautiful aura. Uncover your hidden abilities and enjoy the night's enchantment.
Specialization Years

"Enchanté: The Literary Quest" (Intramurals)

Join amicable competitions ranging from enchanting sonnet duels to quests for the Quill of Wisdom at Lectio Science High School's "Enchanté: The Literary Quest." In this magical celebration of creativity and companionship, explore the enchanted region where fantasy meets literature. Let your magic loose, and may it lead you to triumph!
