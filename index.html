Lectio Science High is a public institution where the pursuit of wisdom, integrity, and profound knowledge define our ethos. We cultivate principled leaders, fostering a community committed to excellence and lifelong learning.

Mission:

Our mission at Lectio Science High School is to build a domain of magical knowledge in which the ethereal dance of mystery and spellmaking coexists with the study of English literature and poetic arts. We hope to transform scholars into enlightened sorcerers by cultivating a deep awareness of the mystical, imaginative, and profound.
