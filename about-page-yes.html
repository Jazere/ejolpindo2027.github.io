About Us:

"Lecto," a humorous compendium of occult information, inspired the establishment of Lectio Science High School in Lilihill in 2025. This enchanting tome, written by three academics, became the school's cornerstone, mixing fun with wisdom.

How Lectio?

Imagine the hamlet bustling as "Lecto" appeared. Curiosity drove the researchers to pour their knowledge into it, resulting in an ethereal campus where scientific investigation met magic. As pupils entered this fantasy environment, a journey occurred that defied traditional schooling. Lectio Science High School evolved into a paradise where whimsy became academic achievement.

Lecto's legacy goes on today, and Lectio Science High School is a monument to its fanciful beginnings—a transforming journey from a mystery book to a refuge of knowledge. At Lectio Science High School, you may join the continuing story of knowledge and magic.
